About

Pentatonix is Tim Corrigan's technology consultancy. We help early stage companies get off the ground by mentoring founders with a focus on technologies, feasibility, Lean Startup methods, team building and growth.

We build businesses and software in an Agile fashion, and have a track record of taking a business idea from concept through to launch and ongoing support.

Tim Corrigan

With 17 years of experience in full stack web and mobile application development, Tim brings extensive technical leadership skills to both startups and established technology companies. He is passionate about delivering high quality, business-relevant software through the use of Agile and Lean methods.

Specialities: Technical management and strategy, systems architecture, integration, mentoring, server side, client side and mobile app development.

Tim speaks at startup events and workshops with a particular focus and passion for demystifying technology.

We have a number of other excellent developers and consultants available for larger projects.

Services

Pentatonix provides startup consultancy, prototyping, programming, systems administration and ongoing support.

Mobile Apps

  • Back End/Database development including hosting infrastructure
